Dole

The ultimate tipping utility is here. Dole includes many features to help you quickly and easily find the ideal amount to tip.

  • Get standard tip rates based on business type
  • Split check evenly or by item
  • Round tip amount by $1 or $0.50
  • Round total amount to the nearest $1
  • Save notes about location for easy later reference
Why struggle with knowing how much to tip? Dole will save you time and give the the confidence to know that you are giving the perfect tip.
